CURATED PHOTOS — Endeman Travel Legacy
=======================================

For each trip, there are two subfolders:

  gallery/   → All your favorite photos from this trip (shown in the gallery section)
  map/       → Exactly 5 photos to show as pins on the map during the presentation
               (GPS location embedded in the photo is ideal, but not required)

HOW TO ADD PHOTOS:
  1. Drag photos directly into the folder — any format (JPEG, HEIC, PNG)
  2. For map/ photos: group shots work best; the photo will appear as a circle
     pin on the map with a leader line pointing to where it was taken
  3. After adding photos, run the scanner to rebuild the gallery:

     python3 scripts/scan_photos.py --curated-only
